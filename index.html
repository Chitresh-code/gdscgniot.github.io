Google Developer Student Clubs
Greater Noida Institute of Technology

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies.

Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining GDSC-GNIOT, students will grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and be able to build solutions and run a community.


  Join on Discord

Concept of GDSC

The GDSC program is a grassroots channel through which Google provides development skills, cloud, mobile and web development skills for students.

Why GDSC?

For students to learn development skills, solve problems through technology and inspire them to be world-class developers and changemakers.

Target audience

GDSC activities are targeted at GNIOT college students and any others including students who want to learn development skills & work to solve real-life problems.

Technologies we're excited about

Opportunities to learn & access deep technical content.


Android Development

Every year Google developers release exciting new updates to the world's most popular operating system. We always have sessions to keep you updated and mastering the latest trends in modern Android development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a member?

The clubs is open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to further their skills. Students of branches other than B.Tech can also join the club.

How frequently do events and workshops occur?

We hold workshops and showcases at Greater Noida Institute of Technology & we would recommend you to join our community by becoming a member to get updates.

What should I carry when attending a workshop?

We recommend you carry with you a notebook, pen and a laptop because more often than not we make our hands dirty with code. Most importantly, carry along a healthy dose of curiosity and enthusiasm.

Who should I reach out to if I have any questions?

If you have any questions or comments, you can reach out on our Discord server or you can mail us by clicking the button below.
